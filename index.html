Arbor

Bioinformatics workflows

The NSF Arbor Workflows project required a portal to create and share data and analyses for bioinformatics researchers and educators. The resulting application, Arbor, enables users to drag and drop data, author and run analytic scripts in R or Python, contruct complex bioinformatics workflows, and visualize the results in the browser.

  • Dates: 2014-2016
  • Client: NSF
  • Resonant components: Girder, Romanesco, GeoJS

Hospital Costs

Interactive visualization

When outcome and cost metrics for thousands of hospitals were released, we sought to answer the question: Does hospital cost relate to the quality of the outcome? Our online tool lets you see cost versus quality, and see how your local hospital stacks up.

  • Resonant components: Tangelo
